About Me

Welcome to the homepage of Andrew Weisbeck! I currently reside in Raleigh North Carolina, but have lived all over the US. I am a Cybersecurity Analyst at Iron Range Cyber for my full-time job and absolutely love what I do. When I am not protecting my clients from cyber threats, I develop web apps, design and host web pages, consult clients in marketing and business strategy, and hunt bug bounties. I am constantly learning to develop my professional career and am currently studying to earn my Comp TIA Security+ certification for my career.

I graduated from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in 2014 with a B.S. in Business Administration and Finance, but that was just the starting point in my continued education. Over the last seven years, I have used my entreprenurial skills and passion for learning to complete freelance work in just about anything you can imagine: SEO Content Creation, Marketing, Website Design, Consulting, and much more. My favorite programming language is Python and I absolutely love spending time playing around with the Kali Linux OS while hunting bug bounties.

I also like the New England Patriots, Kansas City Royals, and my local Carolina Hurricanes. I love gambling (reasonable amounts of money) on pretty much all sports, skiing, spending time with my fiance Lisa and our two kitties, Zulu and Beaux, as well as our lovely Australian Cattle Dog/Staffy Mix Magnolia.
